2 words to know me better

Hello everyone, I'm Michele, a multidisciplinary designer with a focus on UI/UX and interaction design. I worship to browse and look for new solutions to the problems that are presented to users every day with the help of new technologies in order to meet the needs of the user. The study of different cases, the design of solutions and prototyping fascinate me and push me to do better and not to underestimate anything.

I currently work as a freelance UI/UX designer, instead in the past years I have worked in various software houses where I took part in more than 40 national and international projects for clients such as Samsung, Lottomatica, Coop, Colussi, W&H, etc. These professional experiences and my passion have led me to be the designer I am today.
